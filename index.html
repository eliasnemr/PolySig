PolySig.

  • Tips #1 — Inputs

  • To obtain your partner's coins use these functions in your MINI terminal
  • — exportcoin [coinid] - export the proof of a single coin. Share it.
  • — importcoin [data] - Import the proof of a coin.
  • — keepcoin [coinid] - Add a coin to the list of coins you keep track of. MUST be a recent coin in the current blocks.
